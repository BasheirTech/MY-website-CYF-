The iPhone 17, announced by Apple in September 2025, brings a number of upgrades and new features over its predecessors. It features a 6.3‑inch Super Retina XDR OLED display with ProMotion technology, offering a 120Hz adaptive refresh rate and peak outdoor brightness up to 3,000 nits. The screen also supports Always‑On functionality, making it more convenient for glancing at important info.

The design includes an aluminum frame and upgraded Ceramic Shield 2 front glass with improved scratch resistance and anti-reflective coating. It comes in new colors like Black, White, Mist Blue, Sage, and Lavender.

Camera upgrades are notable — the dual rear system includes two 48MP lenses. One acts as a wide lens with 2x optical-quality zoom via cropping, and the other is an ultra-wide. The front-facing camera is now 18MP with a square sensor and Center Stage support for better framing during calls or selfies.

iPhone 17 – Key Features

Display
  • 6.3‑inch Super Retina XDR OLED screen
  • ProMotion technology with adaptive 120Hz refresh rate
Always‑On display
  • Peak brightness up to 3,000 nits for better visibility outdoors
Camera System
  • Dual 48MP rear cameras (main + ultra‑wide)
  • 2x optical-quality zoom via cropping
  • 18MP front camera with Center Stage and improved framing in both portrait and landscape
Performance
  • A19 chip with improved speed and energy efficiency
  • New “N1” wireless chip supports Wi‑Fi 7, Bluetooth 6, and Thread for smart home connectivity
